See that statue over there? That's me. Well, me if was a Greek god and immortalized in stone. In real life, I'm a 21 year old UI designer and software developer from San Diego, CA. Carmel Valley Ninja Camp

View More Info

Carmel Valley Ninja Camp is a summer camp for kids ages 4-12. Campers hone their ninja skills and meet new friends through martial arts adjacent activities such as ziplining, obstacle courses, and much more.

I updated the site with a playful redesign to better emulate the camps brand as well as a responsive navigation for all devices. The site also features a photo gallery, FAQ page, and a fully automated registration page.

Before the creation of this website, the client was taking camper info and payments from the parents all over the phone. Now, registration is completely automated with the registration form, which calculates a price based on the number of children being registered and the length of the camp, then uses Stripe API to securely take credit card info and process payments.
